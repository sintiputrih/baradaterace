Gerardo Ortiz - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-30

Regional Mexican music star who earned a Grammy nomination for his 2010 debut album Ni Hoy Ni Mañana. He has won several Premio Lo Nuestro awards including Norteño Artist in 2013 and both Regional Mexican Album and Song in 2015.

Before Fame

He started making his first public performances at age eight.

Trivia

He survived a violent kidnapping attempt in 2011.

Family Life

He has four brothers: William, Kevin, Anthony and Oscar. His brother Kevin is also a pop singer. He grew up in Sinaloa which is his parents' birthplace.

Associated With

He followed in the footsteps of Larry Hernandez to become a top Mexican-American artist.
