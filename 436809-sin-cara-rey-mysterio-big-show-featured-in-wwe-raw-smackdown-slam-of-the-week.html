Sin Cara, Rey Mysterio &amp; Big Show Featured in WWE Raw &amp; Smackdown Slam of the Week

Publish date: 2024-04-02

WWE Superstar Sin Cara is featured in this week's Raw Slam of the Week, after his big upset victory over former world champion Alberto del Rio. In what has to be more than a coincidence, the title for the video is "Sin Cara returns to old form". The man behind the Sin Cara mask this week was not the original Superstar to play the gimmick, but rather Hunico, or Sin Cara Negro during their brief feud. 

The high flying Rey Mysterio returned to Smackdown this week in the WWE Smackdown Slam of the Week, teaming with the Big Show to take on both members of The Real Americans, Antonio Cesaro and Jack Swagger.
