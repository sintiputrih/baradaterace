Who is James Bender? 

James C Bender is an interior designer known for his contributions to the world of home renovation and design. With a profound passion for transforming spaces and attention to detail, James has established himself as a true visionary in his field. James possesses extraordinary expertise in crafting breathtaking interiors that seamlessly blend functionality, aesthetics, and the distinct personality of each client. His extensive knowledge of design principles, materials, and finishes allows him to create spaces that consistently exceed expectations. James is the founder and owner of an eponymous interior design company based in West Hollywood, CA, established in 2004. With over a decade of experience in the field, James gained recognition through his notable contributions to HGTV's 'House Hunters Renovations,' starting from Season 3 and continuing through Seasons 10 to 17. He subsequently made appearances in the film 'Christina on the Coast,' further solidifying his status as a sought-after designer. In 2022, James showcased a remarkable net worth exceeding $600K, a testament to his exceptional talent and success in the industry. James, 64, has a wealth of experience and expertise that continues to shape his remarkable career. He is married to his partner Roger Ortega.

James Bender's family 

James Bender and Roger Ortega, a loving couple, had a memorable wedding in December 2019, when they walked down the aisle together dressed in black. Their photographer thanked them two years later for capturing the event and described James as one of their favorite people. Roger, born in February 1966, began his career at Blue Cross of California and has been an accountant at Blue Shield of California since September 2006. Their Instagram posts together were discontinued after February 2020, so their current status is unknown. Sadly, his father, James Franklin Bender, a former naval officer, died on May 12, 2015, at the age of 84. However, his mother remains alive and well. James also has a beloved sister, the proud mother of Gracie Whisenhunt, who is now 19. On Gracie's 18th birthday, James expressed his pride and love for his niece on Instagram, wishing her a wonderful journey as she grows into an extraordinary young woman. James Bender's immediate family consists of his mother, sister and niece Gracie.
