Kesha is a chart-topping singer and songwriter. See some of her pictures here!

Kesha was born Kesha Rose Sebert in Los Angeles, California on March 1, 1987. She is well known for her first hit song, ‘TiK ToK,’ which is one of the highest-selling digital singles to date, selling over 14 million copies internationally. She has earned two number-one albums on the US Billboard 200 with ‘Animal’ in 2010, ‘Rainbow’ in 2017, and ‘High Road’ in 2020.

She has attained ten top-ten singles on the US Billboard Hot 100, including ‘Blah Blah Blah’, ‘Your Love Is My Drug’, ‘Take It Off’, ‘Blow’, ‘Die Young’, ‘My First Kiss’ with 3OH!3, ‘Tik Tok’, ‘We R Who We R’, ‘Right Round’ with Flo Rida, and ‘Timber’ with Pitbull. Kesha went through a volatile and very public lawsuit with Dr. Luke beginning in 2014, after she claimed that the producer had sexually assaulted her. Kesha has also written songs for other popular artists, including pop star Britney Spears.
