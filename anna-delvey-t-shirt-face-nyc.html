    Readjusting to the outside world has occupied Anna Delvey for the past few weeks since she was released from ICE custody last month, but the fake German heiress seems to be taking it in her stride.

    Despite not being allowed to post on social media, the 31-year-old has taken great pains to give her fans what they want – a major style moment on the streets of New York City.

    Who is Anna Delvey?

    Also known as Anna Sorokin, the fake German heiress entertained viewers with her utterly unique story of deception that formed the backbone of Netflix's series.

    Anna, who was charged with a combination of offences back in 2019 – some of which included grand larceny in the second degree, attempted grand larceny and theft of services – served nearly four years in prison.

    MORE: Inventing Anna: Where is Anna Delvey now?

    The 31-year-old was released early in February of last year on good behaviour but was arrested by ICE a few weeks later for overstaying her visa. Anna has since been released from custody and she is now living under house arrest in her East Village apartment.

    True to form, she turned heads with her latest outfit, consisting of a puffer maxi coat, chunky centre zip black boots and a pair of oversized round sunglasses. Despite her gallant attempt to blend into her surroundings via her camouflage print outerwear, her jungle colour palette proved no match for the urban NYC backdrop.

    Full of contrasts, Anna also decided to make a statement and rock a T-shirt starring none other than her own face – yes, you read that right.

    The fake heiress is by no means the first notable figure to incorporate her own likeness into her look, with celebrities Paris Hilton, Miley Cyrus, and Nicki Minaj all having flown the flag for the face T-shirt trend over the years (wearing tops featuring their own faces, not Anna Delvey's obvs).

    Clearly Anna is not planning on slinking into the shadows and living her life under the radar anytime soon, not if her rather conspicuous looks are anything to go by.

    Her life choices might be pretty questionable, but we can't help but admire her brazen self-confidence.
