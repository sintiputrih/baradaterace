Vanessa Williams Goes Makeup-Free in a Photo without Filter

Publish date: 2024-04-25

Vanessa Williams from Ugly Betty showed off her ageless beauty as fans gushed over a selfie she posted of herself without any makeup or adding filters.

Vanessa Williams is all about summer and soaking up the sun from her gorgeous patio that overlooks her massive backyard.

While working on her fashion collection with HSN, the former pageant queen has the company of her two Great Danes to help her unwind.

Recently, Williams enjoyed a moment alone on her patio as she shared a photo of herself without any makeup on while displaying a stoic facial expression. In her caption, the actress revealed that she was deep in thought.

As she posed in a black tank top, with her hand cradling the side of her face, she made it clear that her glowing skin in the selfie was not the result of a filter.

Fans couldn't get enough of how stunning she looked. One person gushed, "natural beauty." Another person exclaimed, "still got it! Sexy as hell!"

Other fans called her "beautiful" and "gorgeous." The look even earned her a few new titles with people referring to her as a "diva" and "queen.' Comedian Robin Thede also expressed her adoration for Williams.

Robin Thede commented on a selfie of Vanessa Williams without any make-up on | Source: Instagram.com/vanessawilliamsofficial

In December 2019, Williams shared one of the secrets to her flawless skin as she revealed a product that she always carries when she has to travel for her various work engagements.

The "Ugly Betty" star posted a video showing off her favorite product from ZIIP Beauty. With a layer of gel on her face, she explained how the microcurrent technology worked.

Williams revealed that the product helped tighten her skin. She said it was especially useful for the days she doesn't have the time for a professional facial.

She admitted that at 56-years-old, she uses a lot of different techniques to keep her skin healthy. However, when she shared a photo with her mother on Mother's Day, many fans pointed out that her beauty was definitely genetic.

Along with a selfie of Willaims and her mother looking gorgeous, she expressed her joy in being able to see her amid the COVID-19 pandemic. The actress also sent well wishes to other mothers around the world.
