'The Conners' Latest News and Updates

Publish date: 2024-08-09

The comedy series The Conners is a reboot of the '90s classic Roseanne. Following the firing of Roseanne Barr in 2018, the show was rebranded to focus on the titular working-class family.  

Original cast members John Goodman, Laurie Metcalf, and Sara Gilbert star on the show, alongside newcomers Ames McNamara and Jay R. Ferguson. The Connors is set in Lanford, Ill., and has touched on tough topics such as prescription pill addiction and the coronavirus pandemic.
