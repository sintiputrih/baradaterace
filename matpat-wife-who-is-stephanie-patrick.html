MatPat wife: Who is Stephanie Patrick?

Publish date: 2024-01-21

Matthew Patricka.k.a. One of this generation’s most popular YouTube channels is run by MatPat. MatPat is undoubtedly more than just singular because of his channels The Game Theorists and The Film Theorists, which dissect the darkest secrets in a popular game or the wackiest mythology in movie scenes.

It’s understandable why you won’t be able to move from your seat until you’ve seen at least 20 videos in a row on either channel—his channels simply have so much to offer. MatPat has accumulated enough recognition with his channel The Game Theorists alone to include and host other shows, with over 17.2 million followers alone. He also has The Food Theorists and GTLive in addition to the two stations mentioned above.

MatPat wife: Who is Stephanie Patrick?

In Medina County, Ohio, Matthew Robert Patrick was born on November 15, 1986. While still a student at the esteemed Duke University, he first met Stephanie Cordato, the future wife he would later marry. The two became fast friends while developing the spin-off series known as “The Epic of Stew.” Later, in 2012, the couple exchanged vows in North Carolina. The couple welcomed Oliver, their first child, in July 2018.
