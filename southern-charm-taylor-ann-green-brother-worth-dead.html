Southern Charm star Taylor Ann Greens brother Worth dead at 36 as sister Catie says her family

Publish date: 2024-05-23

SOUTHERN Charm star Taylor Ann Green's brother has died at the age of 36.

The news of the reality celeb's brother Richard Worthington Green's death was announced by the family Monday.

Catie King, sister to Taylor Ann and Richard, known as Worth, paid tribute to her brother on Instagram.

The post revealed that Worth died on Thursday.

"You are the best big brother I could have ever asked for.

"This family will never be the same," she wrote alongside a few photos of Worth smiling from ear to ear.

Catie added: "I love you to the moon and back."

A heartfelt statement was written on the third photo: "You're always loved, never forgotten, forever missed."

Worth's funeral will be held Saturday afternoon in Marion, North Carolina, Catie posted on her Instagram Story.

“Absolutely one of a kind. A pleasure to know and to laugh with." he wrote with a peace sign emoji.

Taylor has yet to comment on her brother's death.

Worth was described as "a beautiful example of how to live life as fully as possible.," in an obituary from Westmoreland Funeral Home & Crematory.

"His personality was vibrant, outgoing, and loving."

The obituary painted Worth to be a family man.

"Worth loved his family dearly," it read. "Any excuse to be with them, he truly loved with his whole heart."

The tribute went on: "He was a vivacious and loving son and grandson, and the best big brother to his sisters, always being there as a protector and a cheerleader."
