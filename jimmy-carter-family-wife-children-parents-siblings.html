Jimmy Carter family, wife, children, parents, siblings

Publish date: 2024-08-20

Jimmy Carter, 98, is an American retired politician who served as the 39th president of the United States from 1977 to 1981. He previously served as a Georgia state senator from 1963 to 1967, and as the 76th governor of Georgia from 1971 to 1975.

In this article, we will dive into his personal life and family to know his wife children, parents and siblings

Table of Content

Jimmy Carter wife

He is a married man who has been married once. he is married to Rosalynn Carter. They’ve been happily married since 1946.

Jimmy Carter children

He is the father of four children who have been identified as Amy Carter, Donnel Carter, Jack Carter and James Carter.

Jimmy Carter parents

He is the son of James Earl Carter Sr. and his wife Lillian Carter.  His father was a successful local businessman, who ran a general store and was an investor in farmland. He had previously served as a reserve second lieutenant in the U.S. Army Quartermaster Corps during World War I. His mother on the other hand was a registered nurse at the Wise Sanitarium, where he was born.

Jimmy Carter siblings

He is one of four children of his parents. He happens to be the first child of his parents, hence, he has three younger siblings who have been identified as Billy Carter, Ruth Carter Stapleton and Gloria Carter Spann. His three siblings are no more. They all died of pancreatic cancer.
