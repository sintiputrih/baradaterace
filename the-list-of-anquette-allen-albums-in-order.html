The List of Anquette Allen Albums in Order

Publish date: 2024-09-05

1. “Throw the P” (1986)
– Featured hit singles: “Janet Reno” and “Get Off Your Ass and Jam”
– Reached number 7 on the Billboard Hot Rap Songs chart
– “Throw the P” is a groundbreaking album that established Anquette Allen as one of the pioneers of Miami Bass music.

2. “I Will Always Be There for You” (1988)
– Included popular tracks like “Ghetto Style” and “Respect”
– Peaked at number 24 on the Billboard Independent Albums chart
– This album showcased Anquette’s versatility as an artist, blending elements of rap, R&B, and soul in her music.

3. “Respect” (1990)
– Notable songs include “Sexual Healing,” a cover of Marvin Gaye’s classic, and “Shake It (Do the 61st)”
– Reached number 23 on the Billboard Hot Rap Songs chart
– Anquette’s “Respect” album further solidified her position as a leading figure in the Miami Bass scene.

4. “Uh-Oh” (1991)
– Featured popular tracks like “Janet Reno” (Remix) and “I Will Always Be There for You” (Remix)
– Debuted at number 7 on the Billboard Heatseekers Albums chart
– “Uh-Oh” showcased Anquette’s ability to experiment with different sounds, incorporating elements of dance and electronic music.

5. “Vintage” (1994)
– Included tracks like “Tear Shit Up” and “Happy Thanksgiving 1994”
– Reached number 14 on the Billboard Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums chart
– With “Vintage,” Anquette returned to her Miami Bass roots, delivering an energetic and infectious album that pleased her dedicated fanbase.

6. “Anquette Singles” (1999)
– Compilation album featuring Anquette’s greatest hits from previous releases
– Included fan-favorite tracks like “Janet Reno,” “I Will Always Be There for You,” and “Respect”
– “Anquette Singles” served as a reminder of Anquette’s impact on the music industry and her ability to consistently produce chart-topping hits.

7. “Legacy” (2022)
– Anquette’s most recent album after a long hiatus from the music industry
– Showcased her growth as an artist, incorporating elements of trap and contemporary R&B
– “Legacy” received critical acclaim and debuted at number 2 on the Billboard Top R&B Albums chart.
