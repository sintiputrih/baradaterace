Journalist and political writer who sits on the editorial board of The Wall Street Journal, where he also publishes a column. He served as the chief speechwriter for George W. Bush from 2006 to 2008. He holds a BA in Philosophy from University of Notre Dame and an MS in Communications from Boston University.

He is a member of the Council on Foreign Relations.

He married Julie Hoffman. They adopted three daughters from China.
