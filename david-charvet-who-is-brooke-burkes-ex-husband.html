David Charvet: Who Is Brooke Burkes Ex-Husband?

Publish date: 2024-08-14

David Charvet is a French singer, actor, model, and television personality. He was born on May 15, 1972, in Lyon, France. He is the son of Christiane Haddad and a businessman, Paul Guez, founder of the Sasson brand of jeans in the 1980s.

Charvet rose to fame in 1992 when he featured on an American television program Baywatch as Matt Brady. He remained on Baywatch for three seasons until 1995. He appeared as Craig Fieldon Melrose Place from 1996-98.

After a successful run at Baywatch and Melrose Place in the 1990s, Charvet decided to return to France with a 5-album contract with Universal Music Group France. His debut single, Should I leave, was a bilingual hit in French and English, hit #3 in France, and also charted in Belgium and Sweden.
