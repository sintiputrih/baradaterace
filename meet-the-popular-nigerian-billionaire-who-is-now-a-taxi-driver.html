Meet The Popular Nigerian Billionaire Who Is Now A Taxi Driver

Publish date: 2024-01-22

A Nigerian man known as Goddy Anabor was once a very wealthy man but now wallows in poverty.

Goddy Anabor, who now works as a taxi driver to makes ends meet was a popular businessman years back known with the nickname as Ikeja axis.

In an exclusive interview with Bodun Kupoluyi, Goddy Anabor opened up on how he lost all his money and went from grace to grass.

Goddy Anabor revealed that he helped three people to become billionaire back in the day but all the people he helped have turned their back on him.

According to the distraught man, he made his riches through multiple investments but lost all of them because the people he entrusted his businesses to mismanaged it and there were also corrupt in their dealings.

Goddy Anabor disclosed that his second wife Anthonia shared business money with people on purchases and supplies.
