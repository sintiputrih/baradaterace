Who is Mac Jones girlfriend? Meet Sophie Scott

Publish date: 2024-02-12

Should Mac Jones hear his name called in the opening round of the 2021 NFL Draft, expect his girlfriend, Sophie Scott, to be cheering him on.

A University of Alabama alum, Scott has been dating Jones — Alabama’s quarterback — since 2019.

“One year with you ❤️ @macjones_10,” Scott gushed on Instagram in September 2020 to mark the couple’s first dating anniversary.

Here’s what you need to know about Jones’ No. 1 fan.

She has an athletic background

A high school soccer player, Scott became a Division 1 recruit but sustained two ACL injuries after graduating from high school in 2018.

She currently works as a physical therapy and occupational therapy aide at the University Orthopaedic Clinic & Spine Center in Tuscaloosa, according to her LinkedIn profile.

She’s also a model and influencer

With 22,000 Instagram followers and counting, Scott regularly posts about her travels and everyday styles.

A brand ambassador for e-tailer Vestique, Scott also partnered with Farm Rich Snacks leading up to the 2021 NFL Draft.

In April 2021, Scott gave her fans a peek at her draft day-style, posting a video of a silver sequin dress and pieces from Kristalize Jewelry.

She’s a game-day staple

In January 2021, Scott had a front-row seat to Jones’ dominant performance in the national title game.

Jones — who has been linked to the San Francisco 49ers — racked up 464 passing yards and five touchdowns in the Crimson Tide’s win over the Ohio State Buckeyes, 52-24.

Scott later joined Jones for post-game celebrations, sharing Instagram clips of the 6-foot-3 athlete on the field at Hard Rock Stadium in Miami.

“That’s my Natty Champ @macjone_10,” she posted.

She celebrated his 2021 NFL Draft declaration

When Jones officially declared for the NFL Draft that month, Scott shared a supportive message to the signal-caller on Instagram.

“@macjones_10 I’m so proud of you for following your dreams and declaring for the 2021 NFL Draft. I’m so excited to see where the next chapter takes us,” Scott wrote at the time.
