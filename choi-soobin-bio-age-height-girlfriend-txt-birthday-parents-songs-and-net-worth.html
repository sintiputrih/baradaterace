Choi Soobin TXT

Soobin is a member of the boy group TXT. In the group, he is a Leader, Vocalist, Rapper, and Dancer. Tomorrow By Together, stylized as TXT is a five-member South Korean boy band. It was formed by Big Hit Music and consists of five members Soobin, Yeonjun, Beomgyu, Taehyun, and HueningKai.

They Made their debut on March 4, 2019, with the EP The Dream Chapter: Star.
