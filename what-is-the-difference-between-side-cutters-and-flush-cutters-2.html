What is the difference between side cutters and flush cutters?

Publish date: 2024-03-07

When would you use each if there is a difference? Answer: The term “flush” means level or straight and on the same plane, so flush cutters cut a wire level. Side cutters, or angle cutters, cut on an angle, meaning the wire edge will be cut to one side.Click to see full answer. In respect to this, what is the difference between side cutters and diagonal cutters?Side cutters are High Leverage Side Cutters. These are your Klein’s or Lineman’s. Diagonal cutters are your Dikes or Snips.Additionally, what are side cutters used for? Side cutters are used to cut wire and the leads (or legs) of components in electronics. They can also be used to strip the insulation from wire. The video below shows small electronic side cutters being used to strip the insulation from the wires of a battery clip. Similarly, it is asked, what is a flush cutter? Flush cutters (wire cutters) Wire cutters that we use in jewellery making are called FLUSH CUTTERS. The aim of the flush cutter is to make a smooth, flat and neat cut in your wire. The FLAT side of the flush cutter gives you this finish.
