Who is Orion Acaba?

Orion Acaba is an American voice actor, who gained fame as the voice of Tiberius Stormwind in the first season of “Critical Role”, a live weekly show in which voice actors play Dungeons & Dragons. Orion is also known as the voice of Piron in the animated series “Gormiti”, and as Apollo Justice in the animated series “Phoenix Wright Ace Attorney: Spirit of Justice” (2016), among other projects on which he has worked so far.

Orion Acaba Wiki- Age, Childhood, and Education

Orion Acaba was born on the 14th October 1980, in Hollywood, California USA; unfortunately, he hasn’t said anything about his parents, but his siblings are Annette and Christal Acaba. He has also remained silent about his education.

Career Beginnings

Before he attained fame with his part in “Critical Role”, Orion had already been in the voice acting world for several years in.

He started in 2009 with the voice of Dracula in the video game “Galactic Bowling”, and the same year worked on several other projects, while in 2012 he was chosen to voice Hoheto Hyga in the animated series “Naruto: Shippuden”, and later that year was the voice of Arathorn in the video game “Guardians of Middle-Earth”. Orion continued to work as a voice actor on numerous video games, including “Neverwinter” (2012), “Disney Infinity” and “Saints Row IV” both in 2013, while he also started providing the voice of Piron in “Gormiti”.

Rise to Prominence

Orion was making progress as a voice actor, and his name became more popular in Hollywood, so 2015 was very fruitful for Orion as he was selected for the show “Critical Role”, heard in 25 episodes of the first season, giving voice to the character, Tiberius Stormwing, and thanks to the enormous popularity of the series, he became a star.

However, he left “Critical Role” after episode No. 27, never to return. He hasn’t spoken about why he left the show, but there were rumors that he took all the money for himself, nevertheless, he is banned from the community and wont be returning.

Regardless, Orion has continued successfully, giving voice to characters from video games including “World of Warcraft: Battle for Azeroth” (2018) as Blackthorn Highmountain”, and Rico Rodriguez in the video game “Just Cause 4”.

He is currently working on the video game “Master X Master (MXM).

Orion has also created an audio series based on his character, Tiberius Stormwind, entitled “Draconian Knights”. He has started a campaign on Kickstarter to raise money for the production of the series.
