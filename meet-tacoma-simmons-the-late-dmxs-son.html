Tacoma’s Father Died When He Was 21 Years Old

Sadly, DMX is no more. The renowned rapper died in 2021 when Tacoma was about 21 years old. On April 2, 2021, DMX was rushed to the White Plains Hospital in New York after suffering a heart attack at his home. It was reported that the heart attack may have been caused by a drug overdose. The following day, his attorney confirmed that DMX was on life support and that the cause of his heart attack was unknown.

Later that night, DMX suffered from cerebral hypoxia, which resulted in oxygen deprivation to the brain, and paramedics attempted to revive him. His former manager, Nakia Walker, reported that DMX was in a vegetative state with lung and brain failure and no current brain activity.

On April 6, DMX’s manager, Steve Rifkind, confirmed that the rapper was in a coma and was to undergo tests on his brain function. Unfortunately, DMX’s death was confirmed on April 9. The rapper’s passing was a great loss to the music community and his fans. It also greatly affected his family, including Tacoma Simmons, who was said to have been close to his father.
