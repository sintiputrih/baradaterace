Mystery 101: Will There Be More Movies Coming To Hallmark in 2022? Heres What One Star Said

Publish date: 2024-09-09

Hallmark Movies & Mysteries is airing a full day of Mystery 101 with Jill Wagner and Kristoffer Polaha.

If you’re unfamiliar, the signature series, which debuted in 2019, centers on college professor Amy Winslow (Wagner), who has become an expert in whodunnits. New in town, Detective Travis Cole (Polaha) arrives on campus to investigate the theft of a genetically engineered plant worth millions from the Life Science Lab, where the two meet.

While Travis is hesitant to involve her in the case, it seems Amy has a knack for solving crimes and aids him in his hunt for a thief and possibly a killer.

Mystery 101 has had seven original movies debut since then, and fans are wondering if there might be more coming.

Click inside to read more…

One of the stars of the series – Kristoffer – gave hope to some fans in the recent weeks about the future of the series.

While chatting with some fans on social media, Kristoffer hinted that another Mystery 101 movie is in the works.

While his response was many weeks ago, we don’t know for sure if this will be happening, but fans can still hope!

You can tune into the Mystery 101 signature movies series, airing all day, Sunday, May 1 on Hallmark Movies & Mysteries.

Kristoffer also has a role in this major, upcoming movie!
