Erinn Bartlett Model

Bartlett first competed in the Miss Massachusetts Teen USA title in 1989 and she emerged as the first runner-up. She went on to win the Miss Massachusetts Teen USA crown in 1991 and represented Massachusetts in the Miss Teen USA 1991 held in Biloxi, Mississippi on August 19, 1991.

She made it to the top twelve of the event that was nationally broadcasted placing sixth in the interview, last in a swimsuit, and tenth in an evening gown. Her average competition score put her in tenth place overall.

Erinn Bartlett Acting

Bartlett made her acting debut in 1999, appearing in the horror film ‘Deep Blue Sea’. The following year, she appeared in the films ‘Little Nicky’, ‘Buying the Cow’ and ‘The In Crowd’. She next had a role in the romantic comedy film ‘Shallow Hal’.

In 2002, Bartlett got featured in the episode “Motherhunt” of the television series ‘A Nero Wolfe Mystery. The same year, she appeared in the romantic dark comedy flick ‘Pumpkin’ as well as in the comedy movie ‘100 Women’.

After this, the actress starred in an episode of ‘Charmed’. She played roles in the comedy-drama movies ‘The Last Run’ and ‘Raising Helen’.She appeared alongside Jennifer Aniston, Shirley MacLaine, Kevin Costner and Mark Ruffalo in Rob Reiner’s ‘Rumor Have It…’ in 2005.

In the year 2006, Bartlett made guest appearances in an episode each of the series ‘How I Met Your Mother’ and ‘Four Kings’. That year, she also featured in the sports-comedy film ‘The Benchwarmers’. In 2007, she guest-starred as Madeline Torrance in ‘NCIS.’

A year later, the actress played Nikki in the episode “Pilot” of ‘Unhitched’. She had a minor role in the series ‘Rules of Engagement in an episode titled “Fountain of Youth” in 2013.
