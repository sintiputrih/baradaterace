Storm Reid Has to Solve Her Own Murder in the Dont Let Go Trailer

Publish date: 2024-03-18

Sometimes when you’re a kid you have to take on more responsibilities than you’re ready for — helping to raise a sibling, getting a job so you can pitch in with the bills, or even solving your own murder to change the future. That last one is the very unfair task that Storm Reid’s Ashley has to take on when she’s killed along with her parents. Only her uncle Jack (David Oyelowo) — who saw her dead body but is now getting phone calls from his living past-niece — can help her stop the crime before it happens again. But also for the first time because time travel is wild! See if they can solve the crime in theaters on August 30.
