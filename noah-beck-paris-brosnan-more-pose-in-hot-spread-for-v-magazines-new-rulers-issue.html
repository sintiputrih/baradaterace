Noah Beck, Paris Brosnan &amp; More Pose in Hot Spread for V Magazine's New Rulers Issue

Publish date: 2024-02-14

Noah Beck and Paris Brosnan are among the “New Rulers.”

The 19-year-old social media star and the 20-year-old model both appear on different digital covers for V Magazine’s “New Rulers” VMAN Issue 46.

Here’s what they had to say…

Paris, on staying inspired in quarantine: “Right now, I’m passionate about my art. I’ve been painting at home all through quarantine and it’s proven to be a great release and outlet for self-expression. It’s nice because I don’t do it for anybody but myself. And if people like it, that’s just a bonus. Everything I paint just comes to mind on the spot. I pull inspiration from the greats, but don’t we all? I’m continuing to learn more and enjoy the process. I hope to do a gallery showing soon.”

Noah, on staying motivated: “I feel blessed to have been able to accomplish the things that I have so far, but I’m by no means complacent. I want to push more into acting, continue to expand my platform, and grow my audience across YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, etc. I want to work in any medium in which I can continue to entertain people and make them smile.”

Other cover stars include 24kGoldn, Damiar Krogh and Xowie Jones, Dylan Geick, Jordun Love and Grimstepper.

For more from all the cover stars, head to VMan.com.
