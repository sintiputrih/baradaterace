Danielle Bregoli Net Worth – $40 million

What is Danielle Bregoli’s net worth?

Introduction

Danielle Bregoli, also known as Bhad Bhabie (pronounced “bad baby”), is an American social media phenomenon and rapper who gained fame after her appearance on Dr. Phil.

Biography

She was born on March 26, 2003, in Boynton Beach, Florida, USA, as Danielle Peskowitz Bregoli. Her mother is of Italian descent and her father is Jewish.

Her parents, Barbara Ann Bregoli and Ira Peskowitz separated when Danielle was an infant.

”Catch me outside”

Bhad Bhabie attends TBT Magazine Social Media Edition Powered By Berman Law at Sway – @Getty

Bregoli appeared with her mother on a Dr. Phil segment called ”I Want To Give Up My Car-Stealing, Knife-Wielding, Twerking 13-Year-Old Daughter Who Tried To Frame Me For A Crime” in 2016.

During the show, Danielle said to the audience – “Cash me outside, howbow dah?”

Her mother later explained that on the streets, ”cash me outside” means she’ll go outside and do what she has to do. The phrase became a viral video meme and catchphrase. Danielle reappeared on Dr. Phil on February 10, 2017.

At the start of 2017, she was signed by Adam Kluger, the CEO of the Kluger Agency.

In July 2017, Bregoli was sentenced to five years’ probation for charges of filing a false police report, marijuana possession, and grand theft.

On August 24, 2017, she released the song ”These Heaux.”

The song peaked at #77 on the Billboard Hot 100. With the release of ”These Heaux,” Bregoli has become the youngest female rap artist to debut on the music chart.

On September 22, 2017, Danielle released the song ”Hi Bich.” The song peaked at #68 on the Billboard Hot 100. At the start of 2018, she uploaded a remix of the song that featured Asian Doll, YBN Nahmir, and Rich the Kid.

On January 31, 2017, she started her YouTube channel (Bhad Bhabie). It has over 7.4 million subscribers and over 1.1 billion views.

On April 14, 2018, Bregoli started her international tour ”Bhanned in the USA Tour.” It ended in July 2019 in Amman, Jordan.

In June 2018, she released another single, called – “Trust Me” feat. Ty Dolla Sign.

In July 2018, she was reportedly interested in buying a property in Tarzana, a neighborhood in the San Fernando Valley region of LA that is valued at around $5 million. 

”Everyone lives with me, so I would have to buy a decent-sized house,” Bregoli said for TMZ. She continued: ”So probably like $5 million, maybe $4 million. I don’t wanna be ridiculous, but I don’t wanna be stingy.”

Debut Mixtape

On September 18, 2018, Bregoli released her debut mixtape, titled -”15” via Atlantic Records. The mixtape featured Ty Dolla Sign, City Girls, YG, Asian Doll, Lil Yachty, and Lil Baby.

Also in 2018, Danielle was nominated for a Billboard Music Award for Top Rap Female Artist.

At the start of 2019, she released two singles: “Bestie” feat. Kodak Black and “Babyface Savage” feat. Tory Lanez.

In February 2019, her reality web series,”Bringing up Bhabie,” was released via Snapchat. Her bodyguard Frank Dellatto and her mother, Barbara Ann, also appeared in the series.

In May 2019, Danielle released a radio version of the song ”Bestie” with Megan Thee Stallion.

In July 2019, she signed a $1 million deal with Pulse Music Group.

In 2020, Bregoli released the songs ”That’s What I Said” and ”Do It Like Me.”

In September 2021, Bregoli launched her own record label, called “Bhad Music.”

So, how much is Danielle Bregoli worth?

Bregoli accrued most of her wealth from selling copies of her singles and mixtape. Her views on YouTube (1.3 billion) made her around $4 million before taxes.

Danielle also made about $10 million from the first season of her reality show, which has over 10 million unique viewers in its first 24 hours. She also makes between $30k and $40k from meet-and-greets events with her fans.

She also has an endorsement deal with Copycat Beauty. According to Refinery29, Danielle received $900,000 from the partnership.

In 2022, Bregoli bought a Florida mansion for $6.1 million. It was an all-cash deal.

In April 2021, Bregoli opened an OnlyFans account. At the time of writing, Danielle earned over $50 million from OnlyFans.

Therefore, Danielle Bregoli aka Bhad Bhabie has an estimated net worth of $40 million.
