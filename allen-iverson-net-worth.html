Allen Iverson Wiki Biography

Allen Ezail Iverson was born on 7th June, 1975 in Hampton, Virginia, USA. He is a former professional basketball player who played in the positions of shooting guard and point guard. As well as many victories in the NBA league, Allen was in the USA men’s basketball team which represented his country in the Athens Olympic Games 2004, where they won bronze medal. Iverson played basketball professionally from 1996 until his official retirement in 2013.
