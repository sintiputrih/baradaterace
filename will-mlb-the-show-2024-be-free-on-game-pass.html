Will MLB the Show 2024 be free on Game Pass?

Publish date: 2024-08-15

In early March 2024, Microsoft unveiled the titles to be added to Xbox Game Pass, and among them is MLB The Show 24. This has sparked curiosity among gaming enthusiasts about whether MLB The Show 2024 will be available for free on Game Pass. Let's delve into the details and analyze this topic comprehensively.

MLB The Show 24 on Xbox Game Pass

Key Information

  • MLB The Show 24, the latest annual baseball game from Sony San Diego and PlayStation Studios, is set to be available on Xbox Game Pass on March 19, 2024, for both cloud and console platforms.
  • Game Pass subscribers have the opportunity to unlock up to four days early access plus deluxe edition bonus content with the purchase of the Digital Deluxe Add-On Bundle for MLB The Show 24.
  • The addition of MLB The Show 24 to Xbox Game Pass forms part of the wave of new games being introduced to the service in March 2024.
  • The game promises players the opportunity to swing for the fences, experience game-deciding moments, become a legend, and live out their baseball dreams.

Previous Releases on Game Pass

  • Historically, MLB The Show titles have been associated with PlayStation platforms, making the availability of MLB The Show 24 on Xbox Game Pass a significant development.
  • The addition of MLB The Show 24 to Xbox Game Pass signals a notable expansion of the game's accessibility beyond its traditional platform.
