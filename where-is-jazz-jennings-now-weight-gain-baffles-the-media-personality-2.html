Where Is Jazz Jennings Now? Weight Gain Baffles The Media Personality

Publish date: 2024-04-01

Where Is Jazz Jennings Now? The new season of the TLC reality show “I Am Jazz” shows her struggle with weight gain. Here is what we know about Jazz so far.

Jennings is an American TV personality, Youtuber, and LGBT rights activist. She hosts “I Am Jazz,” a Youtube series about her life. She also stars in the TLC reality TV series I Am Jazz, which focuses on her family, life as a teenager, and transgender youth. The TLC series first premiered on July 15, 2015, and season 7 of “I Am Jazz” will premiere on November 30 on TLC.

Jazz founded a company, Purple Rainbow Tails, in 2013. She raises money for transgender children with various foundations and charities. She is an honorary co-founder of the company that assists transgender youth. Jennings and her parents founded the company, TransKids Purple Rainbow Foundation in 2007.

Where Is Jazz Jennings Now?

Jazz had plans to attend Harvard University two years ago but deferred to recenter and refocus due to a busy year. In the first look of I Am Jazz Season 7, her parents share their concern on how she will transition to attend Harvard, reports CNN.

Currently, she is ready to take an initiative and create positive changes for her health and body.

Who Is Jazz Jennings Girlfriend Victoria?

Jazz Jennings 100-Pound Weight Gain

Jennings’s mental health issues have led to binge eating and weight gain of almost 100 pounds. Her eating habits have changed due to mental health struggles, which resulted in her weight at 234 pounds. She also addressed her weight gain on her Instagram in June.

Jazz experiences fat-shaming from her family, and it makes her feel humiliated. After gaining all the extra weight, she can no longer do so many things with her body that she used to be able to do. She started binge-eating and gaining weight, and now she is almost 100 pounds heavier, reports Page Six.
