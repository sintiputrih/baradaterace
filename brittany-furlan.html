Brittany Furlan is an American Internet personality, actress, and comedian who was the most followed female video star on Vine until November 2015 and had more than 9.9 million followers by the time Vine ended. In 2015, she was declared one of the most influential people on the Internet by Time. She has appeared in a variety of TV shows and films such as We Are Your Friends, Wrestling Isn’t Wrestling, The Dirt, Spy Intervention, Paradise City, and many more. During the course of her career, she has been nominated for numerous awards such as the Teen Choice Award.
