Who is Brianne Howey?

Brianne Howey is an American film and television actress.

She emerged the popularity after appearing in The Fox supernatural horror television series, The Exorcist (2016), and the Fox thriller series, The Passage (2019).

Presently, she is starring in the lead role of Netflix’s teen series “Ginny & Georgia” as Georgia Miller.

Brianne Howey: Professional Life, Career

Brianne stepped into an acting career in the year 2008 through the short film, Suckerpunch where she was cast in the role of Jess Ramsey.

Afterwards, she appeared in various short films like Appropriatr Sex (2009), Party Favors (2010), Baby Ruth (2011), Backseat Driver (2013), and more.

In the year 2010, Howey made her television debut through the episode The Bachelors of 90210 where she portrayed the role of Stacey. Similarly, she made an appearance in the 2011 police procedural television series, NCIS as a high school girl.

From 2011-to 2012, she portrayed the role of Emily in a sitcom, The Middle.

In 2014, Brianne Howey made an appearance in a black comedy film titled Horrible Bosses 2 as Candy.

Lately, she was cast in the 2019 romantic comedy film, Plus One as Jess Ramsey. The film produces by Jeff Chan and Andrew Rhymer starring Maya Ersjin, Jack Quaid, Perrey Reeves, and other cast members.
