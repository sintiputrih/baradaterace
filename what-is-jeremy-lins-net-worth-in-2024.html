What is Jeremy Lin's Net Worth in 2024?

Publish date: 2024-10-07

Jeremy Lin, the professional basketball player known for his remarkable "Linsanity" period with the New York Knicks, has become a global sensation. As of 2024, his estimated net worth is around $36 million. 

Sources of Wealth

Jeremy Lin's estimated net worth of $36 million in 2024 is attributed to various sources of income, including:

  • Professional Basketball Career: Lin's NBA salary saw significant growth throughout his career, particularly with the Brooklyn Nets, where he signed a three-year contract worth $36 million.
  • Endorsements: "Linsanity" significantly increased Lin's marketability, leading to numerous lucrative endorsement deals that boosted his net worth.

  • Business Ventures: Apart from his basketball earnings, Lin has diversified his income streams through investments and business ventures.

Professional Basketball Career

Jeremy Lin's journey as a professional basketball player began with uncertainty as he went undrafted in the 2010 NBA draft. But his persistence paid off when he signed with the Golden State Warriors, and later with the New York Knicks, where he had his breakout "Linsanity" performances.

His performances not only increased his value in the NBA but also led to more lucrative contracts with teams like the Houston Rockets and the Brooklyn Nets.

International Play and Earnings
