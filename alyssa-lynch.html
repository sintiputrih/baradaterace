Who is Alyssa Lynch? Age, Biography, Net Worth, Height, Boyfriend

Publish date: 2024-09-19

Alyssa Lynch is a 28-year-old Canadian singer, actress and dancer. She is famous for her role s Devon D’Marco in “Project Mc²” and as Rene Bellamy in the TV series “Travelers.”

Profile Summary

Early Life & Background

Alyssa Lynch was born in Langley, British Columbia, Canada on July 7, 1995, to parents whose names and professions are yet to be made public. 

The 5-foot-7-inch tall Canadian actress has a brother named “Hayden Lynch.” Alyssa’s brother Hayden is said to have served as a Senior Content Director at Thrillist.com.

In terms of Alyssa’s education, she attended Affinity Dance School. It was here she developed her passion for dancing. She practiced a lot and competed in dance genres such as Ballet, Hip-Hop, Jazz, Lyrical, and Musical Theatre.

After graduating from Affinity Dance School, she became interested in acting. In order to become an actress she moved to LA where she began to take classes at various studios. 

Profession Career

Lynch’s first job as an actor was when she played the character Tiffani-Amber Thiessen in the movie “The Unauthorized Saved by the Bell Story.”

She played the same role again in the sequel, “The Unauthorized Beverly Hills, 90210 Story.”

However, she is most famous for her roles as Lola in the movie “Before I Fall” and as Rene Bellamy in the TV series “Travelers.”

She has also been in TV shows like “Supernatural” and “Project Mc².” Additionally, she appeared in two music videos: one for The Chainsmokers and another for Hayley Kiyoko.
